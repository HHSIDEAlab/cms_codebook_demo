LABEL:
First Claim Diagnosis E Code Diagnosis Version Code (ICD-9 or ICD-10)
DESCRIPTION:

ICD-10.

future implementation of ICD-10.
On October 1, 2015 the conversion from the 9th version of the International
Classification of Diseases (ICD-9-CM) to version 10 (ICD-10-CM) occurred.
