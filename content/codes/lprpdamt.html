LPRPDAMT
LABEL:
Line Primary Payer (if not Medicare) Paid Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The amount of a payment made on behalf of a Medicare beneficiary by a primary payer
other than Medicare, that the provider is applying to covered Medicare charges for to
the line item service on the non-institutional claim.
