CLM_TYPE
LABEL:
NCH Claim Type Code
DESCRIPTION:
The type of claim that was submitted.
There are different claim types for each major
category of health care provider.
SHORT NAME:
CLM_TYPE
LONG NAME:
NCH_CLM_TYPE_CD
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
2
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
10 =
Home Health Agency (HHA) claim
20 =
Non swing bed Skilled Nursing Facility (SNF) claim
30 =
Swing bed SNF claim
40 =
Hospital Outpatient claim
50 =
Hospice claim
60 =
Inpatient claim
71 =
Local carrier non-durable medical equipment,
prosthetics, orthotics, and supplies (DMEPOS) claim
72 =
Local carrier DMEPOS claim
81 =
Durable medical equipment regional carrier (DMERC);
non-DMEPOS claim
82 =
DMERC; DMEPOS claim
COMMENT:
This variable may not always indicate the type of service performed; for example, when
the claim type code = 60 (inpatient), the services may actually be for post-acute care.
Additional information regarding the type of service on the claim can be found in a CCW
Technical Guidance document entitled: "Getting Started with Medicare data"
