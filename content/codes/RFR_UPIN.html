RFR_UPIN
LABEL:
Carrier/DMERC Claim Ordering Physician UPIN Number
DESCRIPTION:
The unique physician identification number (UPIN) of the physician who referred the
beneficiary or the physician who ordered the Part B services or durable medical
equipment (DME).
NPIs replaced UPINs as the standard provider identifiers beginning in 2007.
The UPIN is
almost never populated after 2009.
