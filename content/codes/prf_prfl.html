PRF_PRFL
LABEL:
Carrier Line Performing Provider ID Number (PIN)
DESCRIPTION:
The provider identification number (PIN) of the physician/supplier (assigned by the
Medicare Administrative Contractor [MAC]) who performed the service for this line
item.
SHORT NAME:
PRF_PRFL
LONG NAME:
CARR_PRFRNG_PIN_NUM
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
15
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
CMS identifies providers using the National Provider Identifier (NPI; effective May 1,
2007), which replaces legacy numbers (UPINs, PINs, etc.) on the standard HIPPA claim
transactions.
