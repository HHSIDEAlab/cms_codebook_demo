FI_NUM
LABEL:
FI or MAC Number
DESCRIPTION:
The identification number assigned by CMS to a fiscal intermediary (FI) authorized to
process institutional claim records.
Effective October 2006, the Medicare Administrative Contractors (MACs) began
replacing the existing fiscal intermediaries and started processing institutional claim
records for states assigned to its jurisdiction.
