CLM_LN
LABEL:
Claim Line Number
DESCRIPTION:
This variable identifies an individual line number on a claim.
Each revenue center record or claim line has a sequential line number to distinguish
distinct services that are submitted on the same claim.
All revenue center records or claim lines on a given claim have the same CLM_ID.
SHORT NAME:
CLM_LN
LONG NAME:
CLM_LINE_NUM
TYPE:
NUM
