LINE_OTHR_APLD_IND_CD5
LABEL:
Line Other Applied Indicator 5
th
Code
DESCRIPTION:
The code used to identify the reason the claim payment amount was adjusted during
claims processing.
SHORT NAME:
LINE_OTHR_APLD_IND_CD5
LONG NAME:
LINE_OTHR_APLD_IND_CD5
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
1
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
A = Gramm-Rudman reduction required for services (03/03/1986-09/30/1986)
B = Interest addition
C = Positive rounding adjustment (due to line item distribution from total claim
reimbursement amount)
D = Negative rounding adjustment (due to line item distribution from total claim
reimbursement amount)
E = Primary Payer allowed charge
F = Good cause
G = PMD Demonstration Reduction
H = Sequestration Reduction Amount
I = eRX Negative Adjustment Reduction Amount
J = ACO Payment Adjustment Amount (Pioneer ACO Reduction) - eff. 1/2014
K = ASC Quality Reporting Payment Reduction (eff. 1/2014)
L = (unknown value)
M = Physician Quality Reporting System (PQRS) Negative Payment Adjustment – eff.
1/2015
N = None
O = Value Modifier Payment Adjustment – eff. 1/2015
P = (unknown value)
Q = Electronic Health Record (EHR) Negative Payment Adjustment – eff. 1/2015
S = Prior Authorization Reduction (eff. 10/2016)
