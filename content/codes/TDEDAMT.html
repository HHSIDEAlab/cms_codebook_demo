TDEDAMT
LABEL:
NCH Inpatient (or other Part A) Total Deductible/Coinsurance Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The total of all Part A and blood deductibles and coinsurance amounts on the claim.
SHORT NAME:
TDEDAMT
LONG NAME:
NCH_IP_TOT_DDCTN_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH QA Process
VALUES:
XXX.XX
COMMENT:
Derivation Rules:
Accumulate the value amounts (from field called CLM_VAL_AMT)
associated with value codes (CLM_VAL_CD) equal to 06, 08 thru 11 and A1, B1 or C1 and
output to this field.
