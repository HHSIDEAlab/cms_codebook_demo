ADMTG_DGNS_CD
LABEL:
Claim Admitting Diagnosis Code
DESCRIPTION:
A diagnosis code on the institutional claim indicating the beneficiary's initial diagnosis at
admission.
This diagnosis code may not be confirmed after the patient is evaluated; it may be
different than the eventual diagnoses (e.g., as in PRNCPAL_DGNS_CD or
ICD_DGNS_CD1-25).
