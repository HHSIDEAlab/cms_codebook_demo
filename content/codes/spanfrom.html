SPANFROM
LABEL:
Claim Occurrence Span From Date
DESCRIPTION:
The from date of a period associated with an occurrence of a specific event relating to
an institutional claim that may affect payer processing.
The first date associated with the claim occurrence span code (variable called the
CLM_SPAN_CD).
