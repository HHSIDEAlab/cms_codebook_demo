MTUS_CNT
LABEL:
Carrier Line Miles/Time/Units/Services (MTUS) Count
DESCRIPTION:
The count of the total units associated with services needing unit reporting such as
transportation, miles, anesthesia time units, number of services, volume of oxygen or
blood units.
This is a line item field on the carrier claim (non-DMERC) and is used for both allowed
and denied services.
SHORT NAME:
MTUS_CNT
LONG NAME:
CARR_LINE_MTUS_CNT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
5
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
For anesthesia (MTUS Indicator = 2) this field should be reported in time unit intervals,
i.e. 15 minute intervals or fraction thereof.
