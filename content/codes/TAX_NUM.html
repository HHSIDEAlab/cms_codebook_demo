TAX_NUM
LABEL:
Line Provider Tax Number
DESCRIPTION:
The federal taxpayer identification number (TIN) that identifies the
physician/practice/supplier to whom payment is made for the line item service.
This number may be an employer identification number (EIN) or social security number
(SSN).
