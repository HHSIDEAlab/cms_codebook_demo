REV_DT
LABEL:
Revenue Center Date
DESCRIPTION:
This is the date of service for the revenue center record.
However, it is populated only for home health claims, hospice claims, and Part B
institutional (HOP) claims.
For home health claims, which are paid based on episodes that can last up to 60 days,
this variable indicates the dates for the individual visits.
