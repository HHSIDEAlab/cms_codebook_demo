LALOWCHG
LABEL:
Line Allowed Charge Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The amount of allowed charges for the line item service on the non-institutional claim.
This charge is used to compute the total claim-level payment to providers or
reimbursement to beneficiaries.
SHORT NAME:
LALOWCHG
LONG NAME:
LINE_ALOWD_CHRG_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
The amount includes both the line-item Medicare and beneficiary-paid amounts (i.e.,
deductible and coinsurance).
