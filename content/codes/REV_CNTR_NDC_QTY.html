REV_CNTR_NDC_QTY
LABEL:
Revenue Center National Drug Code (NDC) Quantity
DESCRIPTION:
Effective with Version 'J', the quantity dispensed for the drug reflected on the revenue
center line item.
SHORT NAME:
REV_CNTR_NDC_QTY
LONG NAME:
REV_CNTR_NDC_QTY
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
10
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
The unit of measurement for the drug that was administered (e.g., grams, liters) is
indicated in the variable called REV_CNTR_NDC_QTY_QLFR_CD.
