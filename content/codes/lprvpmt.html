LPRVPMT
LABEL:
Line Provider Payment Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The payment made by Medicare to the provider for the line item service on the non-
institutional claim.
Additional payments may have been made to the provider -
including beneficiary deductible and coinsurance amounts and/or other primary payer
amounts.
