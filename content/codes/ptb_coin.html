PTB_COIN
LABEL:
NCH Beneficiary Part B Coinsurance Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The amount of money for which the intermediary has determined that the beneficiary is
liable for Part B coinsurance on the institutional claim.
SHORT NAME:
PTB_COIN
LONG NAME:
NCH_BENE_PTB_COINSRNC_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH QA PROCESS
VALUES:
XXX.XX
COMMENT:
Derivation Rules: If value codes (variable called CLM_VAL_CD) = A2, B2 or C2, then the
related value amount (variable called CLM_VAL_AMT ) is output to this field.
