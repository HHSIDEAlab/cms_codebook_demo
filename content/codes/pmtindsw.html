PMTINDSW
LABEL:
Line Payment 80% / 100% Code
DESCRIPTION:
The code indicating that the amount shown in the payment field on the non-
institutional line item represents either 80% or 100% of the allowed charges less any
deductible, or 100% limitation of liability only.
