OT_NPI
LABEL:
Claim Other Physician NPI Number
DESCRIPTION:
On an institutional claim, the National Provider Identifier (NPI) number assigned to
uniquely identify the other physician associated with the institutional claim.
NPIs replaced UPINs as the standard provider identifiers beginning in 2007.
The UPIN is
almost never populated after 2009.
SHORT NAME:
OT_NPI
LONG NAME:
OT_PHYSN_NPI
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
10
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
CMS has determined that dual provider identifiers (old legacy numbers and new NPI)
must be available in the NCH. After the 5/07 NPI implementation, the standard system
maintainers will add the legacy number to the claim when it is adjudicated.
We will
continue to receive the OSCAR provider number and any currently issued UPINs.
Effective May 2007, no new UPINs (legacy numbers) will be generated for new
physicians (Part B and outpatient claims), so there will only be NPIs sent in to the NCH
for those physicians.
