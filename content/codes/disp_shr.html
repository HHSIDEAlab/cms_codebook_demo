DISP_SHR
LABEL:
Claim PPS Capital Disproportionate Share Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The amount of disproportionate share (rate reflecting indigent population served)
portion of the PPS payment for capital.
This is one component of the total amount that is payable for capital PPS for the claim.
The total capital amount, which includes this variable, is in the variable
CLM_TOT_PPS_CPTL_AMT.
SHORT NAME:
DISP_SHR
LONG NAME:
CLM_PPS_CPTL_DSPRPRTNT_SHR_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
XXX.XX
COMMENT:

Commission (MedPAC) documents called “Payment Basics” (see:
