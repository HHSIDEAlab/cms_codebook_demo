CPO_PRVDR_NUM
LABEL:
Care Plan Oversight (CPO) Provider Number
DESCRIPTION:
The National Provider Identifier (NPI) number of the Home Health Agency (HHA) or
Hospice rendering Medicare services during the period the physician is providing care
plan oversight (CPO).
SHORT NAME:
CPO_PRVDR_NUM
LONG NAME:
CPO_PRVDR_NUM
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
10
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
The purpose of this field is to ensure compliance with the CPO requirement that the
beneficiary must be receiving covered HHA or Hospice services during the billing period.
There can be only one CPO provider number per claim, and no other services but CPO
physician services are to be reported on the claim. This field is only present on the non-
DMERC processed carrier claim.
