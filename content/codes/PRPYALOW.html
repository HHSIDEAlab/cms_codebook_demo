PRPYALOW
LABEL:
Line Primary Payer Allowed Charge Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The primary payer allowed charge amount for the line item service on the non-
institutional claim.
If there is a primary payer other than Medicare, there may be an allowed payment for
the provider; if so, this field is populated.
