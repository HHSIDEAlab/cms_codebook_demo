FINL_STD_AMT
LABEL:
Claim Final Standard Payment Amount
DESCRIPTION:
This amount further adjusts the standard Medicare Payment amount (field called
PPS_STD_VAL_PYMT_AMT) by applying additional standardization requirements (e.g.
sequestration).
SHORT NAME:
FINL_STD_AMT
LONG NAME:
FINL_STD_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
XX.XX
COMMENT:
This amount is never used for payments. It is used for comparisons across different
regions of the country for the value-based purchasing initiatives and for research.
It is a
standard Medicare payment amount, without the geographical payment adjustments
and some of the other add-on payments that actually go to the hospitals.
This field is new in October 2014. This field only applies to Inpatient claims.
