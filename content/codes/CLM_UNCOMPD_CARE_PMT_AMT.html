CLM_UNCOMPD_CARE_PMT_AMT
LABEL:
Claim Uncompensated Care Payment Amount
DESCRIPTION:
This field identifies the payment for disproportionate share hospitals (DSH).
It
represents the uncompensated care amount of the payment.

12

These payments were authorized as part of Section 3133 of the Affordable Care Act
(ACA).
