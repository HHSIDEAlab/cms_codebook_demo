PRPAYAMT
LABEL:
NCH Primary Payer (if not Medicare) Claim Paid Amount
DESCRIPTION:
The amount of a payment made on behalf of a Medicare beneficiary by a primary payer
other than Medicare, that the provider is applying to covered Medicare charges on a
non-institutional claim.
SHORT NAME:
PRPAYAMT
LONG NAME:
NCH_PRMRY_PYR_CLM_PD_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH
FILE(S):
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
Derivation Rules:
It is calculated as the sum of the line-level primary payer amounts.
