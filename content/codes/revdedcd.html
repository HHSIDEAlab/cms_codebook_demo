REVDEDCD
LABEL:
Revenue Center Deductible Coinsurance Code
DESCRIPTION:
Code indicating whether the revenue center charges are subject to deductible and/or
coinsurance.
SHORT NAME:
REVDEDCD
LONG NAME:
REV_CNTR_DDCTBL_COINSRNC_CD
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
1
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
0
=
Charges are subject to deductible and coinsurance
1
=
Charges are not subject to deductible
2
=
Charges are not subject to coinsurance
3
=
Charges are not subject to deductible or coinsurance
4
=
No charge or units associated with this revenue center code. (For multiple
HCPCS per single revenue center code)
For revenue center code 0001, the following MSP override values may be present:
M
=
Override code; EGHP (employer group health plan) services involved
N
=
Override code; non-EGHP services involved
X
=
Override code: MSP (Medicare is secondary payer) cost avoided
