CLM_IP_LOW_VOL_PMT_AMT
LABEL:
Claim Inpatient Low Volume Payment Amount
DESCRIPTION:
This is the amount field used to identify a payment adjustment given to hospitals to
account for the higher costs per discharge for low income hospitals under the Inpatient
Prospective Payment System (IPPS).
SHORT NAME:
CLM_IP_LOW_VOL_PMT_AMT
LONG NAME:
CLM_IP_LOW_VOL_PMT_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
XXX.XX
COMMENT:
Payment adjustment for low income IPPS hospitals.
This field was new in 2011.
