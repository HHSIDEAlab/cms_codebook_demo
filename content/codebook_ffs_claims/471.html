PRF_UPIN
LABEL:
Carrier Line Performing UPIN Number
DESCRIPTION:
The unique physician identification number (UPIN) of the physician who performed the
service for this line item on the carrier claim (non-DMERC).
NPIs replaced UPINs as the standard provider identifiers beginning in 2007.
The UPIN is
almost never populated after 2009.
