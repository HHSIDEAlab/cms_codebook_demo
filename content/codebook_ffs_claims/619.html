SUPLRNUM
LABEL:
DMERC Line Supplier Provider Number
DESCRIPTION:
The billing number assigned to the supplier of the Part B service/DMEPOS by the
National Supplier Clearinghouse, as reported on the line item for the DMERC claim.
SHORT NAME:
SUPLRNUM
LONG NAME:
PRVDR_NUM
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
10
SOURCE:
NCH
FILE(S):
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
Different types of identifiers may be used.
Refer to the variable called
DMERC_LINE_SUPPLR_TYPE_CD to determine the type used for each line.
