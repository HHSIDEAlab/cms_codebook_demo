EXPNSDT2
LABEL:
Line Last Expense Date
DESCRIPTION:
The ending date (last expense) for the line item service on the non-institutional claim.
It is almost always the same as the line-level first expense date (variable called
LINE_1ST_EXPNS_DT); exception is for DME claims - where some services are billed in
advance.
