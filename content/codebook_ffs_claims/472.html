PRFNPI
LABEL:
Carrier Line Performing NPI Number
DESCRIPTION:
The National Provider Identifier (NPI) assigned to the performing provider.
SHORT NAME:
PRFNPI
LONG NAME:
PRF_PHYSN_NPI
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
-
COMMENT:
Effective May 2007, the NPI became the national standard identifier for covered health
care providers.
NPIs replaced the legacy numbers (UPINs, PINs, etc.) on the standard
HIPPA claim transactions.
The UPIN is almost never populated after 2009.
