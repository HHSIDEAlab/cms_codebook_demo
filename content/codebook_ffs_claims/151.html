CLM_TRTMT_AUTHRZTN_NUM
LABEL:
Claim Treatment Authorization Number
DESCRIPTION:
The number assigned by the medical reviewer and reported by the provider to identify
the medical review (treatment authorization) action taken after review of the
beneficiary's case. It designates that treatment covered by the bill has been authorized
by the payer.

CLM_TRTMT_AUTHRZTN_NUM
