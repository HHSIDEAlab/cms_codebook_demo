PER_DIEM
LABEL:
Claim Pass Thru Per Diem Amount
DESCRIPTION:
Medicare establishes a daily payment amount to reimburse IPPS
hospitals for certain
“pass-through” expenses, such as capital-related costs, direct medical education costs,
kidney acquisition costs for hospitals that are renal transplant centers, and bad debts.
This variable is the daily payment rate for pass-through expenses.
It is not included in
the CLM_PMT_AMT field.
To determine the total of the pass-through payments for a hospitalization, this field
should be multiplied by the claim Medicare utilization day count
(CLM_UTLZTN_DAY_CNT).
Then, total Medicare payments for a hospitalization claim
can be determined by summing this product and the CLM_PMT_AMT field.
