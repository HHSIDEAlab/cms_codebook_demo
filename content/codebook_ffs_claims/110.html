DESCRIPTION:
The field identifies the claims that qualify for specific claims processing edits related to
benefit enhancement through the Next Generation (NG) Accountable Care Organization
(ACO).
SHORT NAME:
CLM_NEXT_GNRTN_ACO_IND_CD2
LONG NAME:
CLM_NEXT_GNRTN_ACO_IND_CD2
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
1
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
0 = Base record (no enhancements)
1 = Population Based Payments (PBP)
2 = Telehealth
3 = Post Discharge Home Health Visits
4 = 3-Day SNF Waiver
5 = Capitation
COMMENT:
There are 5 of these ACO fields (CLM_NEXT_GNRTN_ACO_IND_CD1 -
CLM_NEXT_GNRTN_ACO_IND_CD5).
