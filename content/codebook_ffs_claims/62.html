BENE_ID
LABEL:
Encrypted CCW Beneficiary ID
DESCRIPTION:
The unique CCW identifier for a beneficiary.
The CCW assigns a unique beneficiary identification number to each individual who
receives Medicare and/or Medicaid, and uses that number to identify an individual’s
records in all CCW data files (e.g., Medicare claims, MAX claims, MDS assessment data).
This number does not change during a beneficiary’s lifetime and each number is used
only once.
The BENE_ID is specific to the CCW and is not applicable to any other identification
system or data source.
