RNDRNG_PHYSN_UPIN
LABEL:
Revenue Center Rendering Physician UPIN
DESCRIPTION:
This variable is the unique physician identification number (UPIN) for the physician who
rendered the services on the revenue center record.
NPIs replaced UPINs as the standard provider identifiers beginning in 2007.
The UPIN is
almost never populated after 2009.
