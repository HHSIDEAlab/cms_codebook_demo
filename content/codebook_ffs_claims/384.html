MCOPDSW
LABEL:
Claim MCO Paid Switch
DESCRIPTION:
A switch indicating whether or not a Managed Care Organization (MCO) has paid the
provider for an institutional claim.
SHORT NAME:
MCOPDSW
LONG NAME:
CLM_MCO_PD_SW
TYPE:
CHAR
LENGTH:
1
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
Blank = No managed care organization (MCO) payment
0 = No managed care organization (MCO) payment
1 = MCO paid provider for the claim
COMMENT:
-
