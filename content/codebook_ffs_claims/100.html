CLM_FULL_STD_PYMT_AMT
LABEL:
Claim Full Standard Payment Amount
DESCRIPTION:
This variable is the standard payment amount for long-term care hospitals (LTCH) under
the Medicare prospective payment system (PPS), which is based on the MS-LTC-DRG.
This amount does not include any applicable outlier payment amount.
SHORT NAME:
CLM_FULL_STD_PYMT_AMT
LONG NAME:
CLM_FULL_STD_PYMT_AMT
TYPE:
NUM
LENGTH:
12
SOURCE:
NCH
VALUES:
XXX.XX
COMMENT:
Applies only to Inpatient (LTCH) claims.
This field is new in October 2015.
For a LTCH PPS claim, only one of four fields will be populated
(CLM_SITE_NTRL_PYMT_CST_AMT, CLM_SITE_NTRL_PYMT_IPPS_AMT,
CLM_FULL_STD_PYMT_AMT, or CLM_SS_OUTLIER_STD_PYMT_AMT) as they are
mutually exclusive (i.e., only one of the 4 fields will have a non-zero value). The field
with the non-zero value is included in the Claim Payment Amount field.
